Megan O. Kelly

megan.kelly@uwaterloo.ca

Hello! I am a PhD Candidate at the University of Waterloo (Ontario, Canada) where I also completed my MA in Cognitive Psychology.

I am interested in the relation between internal memory and the many external memory supports available to us (e.g., notebooks, phones, the internet, and AI). I focus on a variety of subtopics within traditional memory research while trying to better understand everyday "cognitive problems" such as losing one's phone and, as a likely result, losing much of the information contained within it!

In my spare time, I enjoy tending to my plants, meeting pets (pictures are better than nothing!), and trying new restaurants.
