Megan O. Kelly

I am a PhD Candidate at the University of Waterloo (ON, Canada) interested in the relations between memory, beliefs about memory, and the cognitive supports around us (e.g., notebooks, the internet, and AI).

Research

I focus on a variety of subtopics within traditional memory research while trying to better understand everyday "cognitive problems" such as losing one's phone and, as a likely result, losing much of the information contained within it!
