Megan O. Kelly

I am a PhD Candidate at the University of Waterloo (ON, Canada) interested in the relations between memory, beliefs about memory, and the use of cognitive supports (e.g., notebooks, the internet, and AI). I focus on a variety of traditional memory topics in trying to better understand "everyday" memory problems.
